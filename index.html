❓ How to Play

💰 Making Money

Tap the big 💰 HUSTLE button to earn money manually. The amount shown below it is what each click earns right now.

Buy Businesses in the bottom-left panel — they earn money automatically over time. The progress bar shows when the next payout fires.

Your $/sec in the top bar is purely from businesses. Clicking doesn't count toward it.

💡 Early game: click a lot. Late game: businesses take over and clicking barely matters unless you stack click buffs.

🏢 Businesses

Each business earns money automatically on a cycle timer. When the cycle bar at the bottom of a business fills up, it pays out and immediately restarts — there is no pause between cycles.

The bar does not freeze after a payout. What looks like a brief freeze is just the bar resetting to near-zero (the tiny leftover from the last cycle) and starting to fill again. This happens in a single frame — the restart is instant. Keep in mind the visual bar may not always reflect what you are actually earning — money is being added to your total even during that split-second reset, so the income is never interrupted even if the bar briefly looks like it stopped.

Key stats per business: base cost to buy, base income per cycle, and cycle interval in seconds. Owning more of the same business multiplies the payout — 10 Lemonade Stands pays 10x what one does.

Speed perks (Speed Demon, Turbo Business) and Speed Demon tree nodes shorten the cycle interval, making businesses pay out more times per second. Income perks (Empire Builder, Double Down, Monopoly) multiply the payout amount without changing the speed.

💡 Buying upgrades specific to a business (e.g. Catchy Sign for Lemonade Stand) can multiply that business's income by 2–20x — often more valuable than buying more of the same business.

⬆️ Upgrades vs Perks

Upgrades (2nd panel) are one-time purchases with money. They multiply a specific business or your click value permanently. Most require you to own at least one of that business first.

Perks (4th panel) are also bought with money, permanent, and affect broader things like frenzy cooldown, passive income, or click speed.

Combo Strike in Upgrades unlocks the combo multiplier — without it, clicking gives no combo bonus at all.

💡 Upgrades reset on prestige. Perks also reset. Only Skill Tree nodes survive prestige.

🔥 Clicks vs Income

Click buffs only affect what you earn by tapping the button. Useless if you're AFK.

Income buffs multiply what all your businesses earn passively. More valuable the more businesses you own.

Both buffs (like Gold Rush) multiply everything at once — best value.

💡 Early game a click buff is great. By mid-game, an income buff is almost always better.

⚡ Consumables

Consumables are one-use temporary boosts bought with money. They go on cooldown after use.

All consumables start locked. Buy Consumables I–V in the Upgrades panel to unlock each tier. Tier 1 is cheap ($500), Tier 5 costs $10B and is prestige-era only.

Tiers go 1→5. Higher tier = stronger effect, higher cost, longer cooldown. Don't buy tier 4–5 items until late game.

💡 Time Warp fires all business payouts instantly — great to combine with an income buff. Omega Warp and The Big One are tier 5 and pay out 100x and a full week of income.

🔥 Combo Multiplier

The COMBO meter builds as you click repeatedly. Higher combo = more money per click.

It decays if you stop clicking. The bar shows how long before it starts falling.

You must buy Combo Strike in the Upgrades panel ($200) to unlock it. Until then it does nothing.

💡 Unlock "Combo Speed I" in the Skill Tree to build combo faster. "Combo Strike" in the Skill Tree lets you lock your combo at max.

⬡ Hustle Tokens

Tokens are the prestige currency — you earn them by prestiging. Prestige unlocks automatically at $1B total earned.

Tokens use a cube root (∛) formula: 8 × ∛(earned ÷ $1B). Cube root means the scale grows very slowly — earning 8x more money only gets you 2x more tokens. This keeps each prestige feeling meaningful without inflating token counts.

$1B earned → 5 tokens
$4B earned → 10 tokens
$25B earned → 25 tokens
$100B earned → 50 tokens
$1T earned → ~160 tokens
$10T earned → ~500 tokens

Spend tokens in the Skill Tree 🌳 on permanent upgrades that survive every prestige.

💡 Token Miner nodes in the tree multiply your token yield per prestige. E.g. Token Miner III (10x) at $100B gives 500 tokens instead of 50.

✨ Prestige

Prestige resets your money, businesses, upgrades, and perks — but keeps your Skill Tree nodes and earns you tokens.

Prestige unlocks automatically when your total all-time earnings reach $1 Billion — this is NOT your current cash balance. It counts every dollar you've ever earned that run, even money you've already spent on businesses and upgrades.

Each prestige earns tokens based on how much you made that run (see the ⬡ Hustle Tokens section for the full breakdown) and grants a permanent income multiplier that stacks — prestige 1 = 1.5x, prestige 2 = 2.25x, etc.

💡 The top bar shows your progress toward $1B before unlock, then shows "+X tokens" once unlocked. Wait until the token count is worth it before prestiging.

🌳 Skill Tree

The Skill Tree is the only thing that permanently improves your game across prestiges. Everything else resets.

Nodes unlock from top to bottom — you must buy parent nodes first. Locked nodes are shown faded.

Costs range from 5⬡ to 250⬡ (GODMODE). GODMODE unlocks everything and doubles all bonuses.

💡 Hover over any node to see what it does before buying. Greyed out = can't afford or missing requirement.

⚡ Frenzy & Mega Frenzy

Frenzy gives 5x click earnings for 8 seconds. Unlock it via "First Steps" in the Skill Tree.

Mega Frenzy gives 15x click earnings for 6 seconds. Unlock via "Mega Frenzy" node in the tree.

Both have cooldowns. The "Frenzy Auto" and "Mega Auto" tree nodes make them activate automatically.

💡 Stack a click consumable + Frenzy + Mega Frenzy at the same time for massive burst damage.

📊 Milestones & XP

The milestone bar tracks your progress toward total money earned targets. Hitting one spawns a shockwave.

XP (top right bar) levels you up as you earn money. Your title changes with each level — purely cosmetic.

💡 Milestones reset on prestige. XP and level also reset. Only Skill Tree and tokens persist.

💵 Money Chains

A Money Chain is a bonus event that spawns automatically. The 💵 Chain bar in the top-left fills at 0.5% per second. The bar doesn't trigger the chain at 100% — instead, every second the game rolls the current bar percentage as a spawn chance. So at 40% there's a 40% chance that second, at 80% an 80% chance. In practice chains usually spawn when the bar is around 70–90% full.

When it spawns, a glowing 💵 button appears on one side of the screen. Click it and the next appears at a random spot on the same side. Hit all 5 buttons within 4 seconds each to complete the chain and collect the 💰 payout. Previous buttons stay faded with a dashed line connecting them.

Payout scales with your progress — roughly 2.5 minutes of passive income, or 60 clicks worth if that's higher, multiplied by your prestige bonus.

💡 The timer resets after each chain (complete or failed) and only runs while the game is open.

💾 Save / Load

📤 Export / Import save string
Copy your save string to back it up or move it to another device. The string is tamper-protected — edited strings will be rejected.
⚠️ Are you sure?
This will reset your current run — money, businesses, upgrades, skill tree, tokens, and active progress. Your manual save slots will NOT be deleted so you can reload them anytime.

💾 Saving Your Game

Click 💾 Save in the top-right to open the save menu. You have 5 manual save slots — each shows your level, total earned, and prestige count.

Same browser, same device: saves persist in your browser's local storage. Return to the same code.org link in the same browser and your saves will be there.

Different device or browser: use Export to copy a save string, then Import on the other device to paste it in.

Update-safe saves: saves are stored by name, not position — so if the game gets updated with new businesses, upgrades, or tree nodes, your old save still loads correctly. New items just start at zero.

Tamper protection: the export string has a hidden checksum. If you edit it to change your money or tokens, the checksum won't match and the import is rejected.

💡 Autosave runs every 60 seconds. If you return with no manual saves, it automatically restores your last session.
